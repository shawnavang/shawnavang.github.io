GIMM 110 Individual Game - This Little Crow

This Little Crow was a simple platformer game where the player controlled a small crow navigating through various obstacles. The objective was to collect small pieces of jewelry while avoiding the explosives. Created in Unity featuring physics-driven gameplay, including moving platforms, power-ups, and a dash mechanic, with core systems developed in C#.






GIMM 250 - Schroedinger's Witness

Schrödinger’s Witness is an interactive comic developed by a team of four, inspired by the concept of Schrödinger’s Cat. It blends narrative and gameplay as players uncover the mystery of Amelia Brookstone, where each discovered clue can shift the outcome between life and death. Built in Unity with C#, the project features my work as the lead artist, shaping its visual style and storytelling experience.
